About

EcoReward Foundation is a technology-driven environmental initiative that encourages individuals to actively participate in saving the planet 🌱.


Our platform empowers users to upload images of waste materials and instantly learn whether they are recyclable, biodegradable, or non-recyclable, promoting better waste management habits.


We combine awareness with action by introducing a reward-based system where users earn points for eco-friendly activities and climb the leaderboard 🏆.


Our vision is to build a global community where sustainability becomes a habit, not a choice 🌍.

Our Work

We actively create impact through both real-world actions and digital innovation:

🧹 Clean Drives

We organize community cleaning campaigns to remove waste from public areas and promote hygiene awareness.

🌳 Tree Plantation

We plant trees to improve air quality, reduce pollution, and restore ecological balance.

♻️ Recycling

We educate individuals on proper waste segregation and recycling practices for sustainable living.

🏆 Rewards

Users upload eco-friendly activities and earn points, making sustainability engaging and fun.
